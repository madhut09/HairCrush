About Hair Style

Hair crush is a Hair Extensions specialist Salon. We offer 3 techniques hair extensions: tape in, keratin bonds and micro ring with additional services. As specialists in hair extensions, we only provide the highest quality human hair using the latest, damage-free attachment techniques. We spend time and effort in getting your look perfect, so you can get the hair you have always dreamed of.

We keep all types, colours and lengths of hair in stock, and customise each extension specifically to our clients needs, to ensure they get perfectly colour matched, natural looking hair.

We offer a free consultation for you to come into the salon to show you hair samples, discuss the different methods and to which is most suitable for your natural hair type and colour match.

Our Hair Extensions!

Our hair extensions are made from 100% natural Remy hair with all cuticles intact, so the hair stays protected and lasts much longer than other extensions in the market. Our hair is untreated, unprocessed, and is not adulterated with synthetic fibers or animal hair. We don't use any harsh chemicals or silicones, making our products very safe and comfortable for everyday use.

Our Products

Patches

Patches are a great combination for people who have a similar pattern of thinning and don’t want to use a topper. These patches come individually and not in a pair.

Clip-in

Clip-In Extensions are the easiest path to getting longer and fuller hair. The Clip-In Extensions are practically DIY, with no long-term bonding to your hair. The Clip-In hair pieces can be fixed to your hair in two easy steps—just Snap-On and Snap-Off!

Volumizers

Increase your hair volume within seconds using our Volumizers whih increases volume instanly. Volumizers come in standard volumes which increase proportionately according to the length!

Ponytail

Our Wrap Around Ponytail Extensions are made of 100% real human hair that you can easily attach and remove with the help of handy velcro and a small hidden comb. Colour it, bleach it, use heat on it and style them in whatever way you like. These extensions will last you for years!
